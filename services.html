Our Services

BANT MARKETING

BANT, an acronym that stands for “Budget, Authority, Need, Timing” is a sales strategy which we provides a simple framework for qualifying prospects in a business-to-business (B2B) sales setting. We as an organization evaluate whether, and to what degree.

SQL MARKETING

A sales-qualified lead (SQL) is a prospective customer who has moved through the sales pipeline – from marketing-qualified lead through sales-accepted lead – to a position where our sales team can now work on converting them into an active customer.

Appointment Generation

Appointment Generation is the supplement your Sales Team needs in order to convert potential clients into customers. We connect with your potential clients to generate an appointment with your sales personnel so that they can then go forward and close the deal!

MARKETING QUALIFIED LEAD

A marketing-qualified lead (MQL) is a potential customer that has been reviewed by the marketing team and satisfies the criteria necessary to be passed along to the sales team. Our team then focuses on targeting professionals like these to then convert into sales

Account Based Marketing

Account-based marketing (ABM), also known as key account marketing, is a strategic approach to business marketing based on account awareness in which an our organization considers and communicates with individual prospect or customer accounts as markets of one.


CONFIRMED CALL BACK

Confirmed Call Back or (CCL) is a service which we provide as an organization for marketing and sales programs. In this approach we provide your business with a list of potentials clients who have requested a call back from your experts to be enlightened by your service or product.

EMAIL MARKETING

Email marketing is a direct marketing channel that our organization provides that lets businesses share new products, sales, and updates with customers on their contact list. Its high ROI makes it crucial to most businesses’ overall inbound strategy.

Content Syndication

Similar to Email marketing, Content Syndication focusses on syndicating newletters, documents, information a business wants to syndicate to their contact clients. We ensure the receival of your business’s content by your client and are provide analysis on the reaction of clients based on our reports.

DATA CLEANSING

Data cleansing is the process of fixing or removing incorrect, corrupted, incorrectly formatted, duplicate, or incomplete data within a dataset. We provide Prospect Verification as well as Email verification under this umbrella of Data Cleansing to make sure your Contact Data list always stays up to-date and in term boosts ROIs.
